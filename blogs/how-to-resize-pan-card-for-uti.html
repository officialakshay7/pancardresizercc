1. Exact PAN Card Photo Size for UTIITSL

The UTIITSL portal has specific requirements for photographs. Understanding the uti pan card photo size is crucial for successful application submission.

📸 Official UTIITSL Photo Specifications:

  • Pixel Dimensions: 213 x 213 pixels (square format)
  • Resolution: 300 DPI (Dots Per Inch)
  • Physical Size: 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm
  • File Format: JPEG/JPG only
  • File Size: 20 KB to 50 KB
  • Background: Plain white only
  • Face Coverage: 70-80% of the frame

Many applicants make the mistake of uploading larger images thinking they can be compressed later. However, UTIITSL's system checks both dimensions and DPI. A 213x213 pixel image at 72 DPI will be rejected because the physical size calculation would be incorrect for printing on the PAN card.

Why Does UTI Require 213x213 Pixels?

The 213x213 pixel dimension at 300 DPI ensures that when printed on the physical PAN card, the photo maintains proper quality and dimensions. This specific size was chosen by the Income Tax Department to ensure uniformity across all PAN cards issued in India. Our UTI pan card resizer automatically handles these calculations for you.

2. PAN Card Signature Size for UTIITSL

The pan card signature size for UTI is equally important. UTIITSL has specific requirements for signatures to ensure clarity when printed on the physical card.

✒️ Official UTIITSL Signature Specifications:

  • Pixel Dimensions: 400 x 200 pixels (rectangular)
  • Resolution: 600 DPI (higher than photo for clarity)
  • Physical Size: 4.5 cm x 2.0 cm
  • File Format: JPEG/JPG only
  • File Size: 10 KB to 20 KB
  • Ink Color: Black ink on white background only
  • Style: Normal signature (not capital letters)

The higher 600 DPI requirement for signatures ensures that the fine lines of your signature remain crisp when printed. A lower resolution might make the signature appear blurry or pixelated on the physical card, which could cause verification issues.

How to Get the Right Signature for UTI

For best results with UTI applications:

  • Use a black pen on plain white paper (no colored inks)
  • Sign within a box of approximately 5 cm x 2.5 cm
  • Scan at 600 DPI or take a clear photo with good lighting
  • Ensure no shadows, creases, or folds in the paper
  • Avoid using decorative elements or multiple colors
  • Make sure your signature fits entirely within the frame

Method 2: Manual Resizing Using Software for UTI

If you prefer using photo editing software like Photoshop, GIMP, or MS Paint for UTI applications:

  1. Open your image in the photo editor
  2. Go to Image Size or Resize settings
  3. For UTI photo: Set width to 213 pixels, height to 213 pixels
  4. For UTI signature: Set width to 400 pixels, height to 200 pixels
  5. Set resolution to 300 DPI for photos, 600 DPI for signatures
  6. Ensure "Resample" or "Maintain quality" is checked
  7. Save as JPEG with quality setting around 80-85% to achieve 20-50KB file size for photos
  8. For signatures, use slightly lower quality (75-80%) to stay within 10-20KB

5. UTI Background and Visual Requirements

UTIITSL has strict visual requirements to ensure the PAN card is professional and meets government standards.

UTI Photo Background Guidelines:

  • Color: Pure white only (#FFFFFF) - no off-white or cream
  • No shadows: Even lighting across the face, no shadows on background
  • No objects: No chairs, patterns, textures, or other people visible
  • No headgear: Except for religious reasons (face must be fully visible from chin to forehead)
  • Glasses: Allowed if no glare, frames not covering eyes, and lenses are clear
  • Expression: Neutral expression with mouth closed

UTI Signature Guidelines:

  • Ink: Black only on white paper - no blue, red, or other colors
  • Placement: Full signature within the box, not touching edges
  • No alterations: Don't use digital fonts, typed signatures, or stamps
  • Consistency: Should match your bank records and other official documents
  • Size: Signature should be large enough to be clear but fit within 4.5 cm x 2.0 cm area
