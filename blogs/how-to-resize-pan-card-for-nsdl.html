1. Exact PAN Card Photo Size for NSDL

The NSDL (Protean) portal has specific requirements for photographs. Understanding the pan card photo size is crucial for successful application submission.

📸 Official NSDL Photo Specifications:

  • Pixel Dimensions: 213 x 213 pixels (square format)
  • Resolution: 300 DPI (Dots Per Inch)
  • Physical Size: 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm
  • File Format: JPEG/JPG only
  • File Size: 20 KB to 50 KB
  • Background: Plain white only
  • Face Coverage: 70-80% of the frame

Many applicants make the mistake of uploading larger images thinking they can be compressed later. However, NSDL's system checks both dimensions and DPI. A 213x213 pixel image at 72 DPI will be rejected because the physical size calculation would be incorrect.

Why 213x213 pixels at 300 DPI?

The calculation is straightforward: 213 pixels ÷ 300 DPI = 0.71 inches = 1.8 cm (width). Wait, that doesn't match 3.5 cm? Let's clarify: The 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm is the printed photo size on the PAN card. The digital requirement of 213x213 pixels at 300 DPI ensures that when printed, the photo maintains proper quality and dimensions. The square format (213x213) actually allows for a slightly cropped version of your 4.5 cm height photo.

2. PAN Card Signature Size for NSDL

The pan card signature size is equally important. NSDL has specific requirements for signatures to ensure clarity when printed on the physical card.

✒️ Official NSDL Signature Specifications:

  • Pixel Dimensions: 400 x 200 pixels (rectangular)
  • Resolution: 600 DPI (higher than photo)
  • Physical Size: 4.5 cm x 2.0 cm
  • File Format: JPEG/JPG only
  • File Size: 10 KB to 20 KB
  • Ink Color: Black ink on white background
  • Style: Normal signature (not capital letters)

The higher 600 DPI requirement for signatures ensures that the fine lines of your signature remain crisp when printed. A lower resolution might make the signature appear blurry or pixelated on the physical card.

How to Get the Right Signature

For best results:

  • Use a black pen on plain white paper
  • Sign within a box of approximately 5 cm x 2.5 cm
  • Scan at 600 DPI or take a clear photo
  • Ensure no shadows or creases in the paper
  • Avoid using colored inks or decorative elements

5. Background and Visual Requirements

NSDL has strict visual requirements to ensure the PAN card is professional and verifiable.

Photo Background Guidelines:

  • Color: Pure white only (#FFFFFF)
  • No shadows: Even lighting across the face
  • No objects: No chairs, patterns, or other people visible
  • No headgear: Except for religious reasons (face must be fully visible)
  • Glasses: Allowed if no glare, frames not covering eyes

Signature Guidelines:

  • Ink: Black only on white paper
  • Placement: Full signature within the box
  • No alterations: Don't use digital fonts or typed signatures
  • Consistency: Should match your bank records if possible
