📖 Our story

It started in early 2025. A friend was applying for a PAN card, but every pan card resizer they found either uploaded images to unknown servers, asked for an email, or was filled with intrusive ads. They just wanted to resize a photo to 213x213 and a signature to 400x200 - quickly and safely.

We realised there was no straightforward, private tool for such a common Indian document. So we built one. No backend, no databases, no hidden catches. Just a few lines of JavaScript that run in your browser. That's how PanCardResizer.cc was born.

Today, the tool helps over 10,000 users daily - from students and CA aspirants to CSC operators and busy professionals. And we've kept our promise: zero uploads, zero storage, and the exact dimensions needed for NSDL and UTIITSL portals.

Privacy first, always

We don't want to see your face or your signature. That's why the pan card photo resize happens 100% client‑side. No server, no sneak peeks.
